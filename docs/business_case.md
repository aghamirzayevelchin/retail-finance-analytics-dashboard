# Business Case Study

## Problem
Retail company management cannot clearly track profitability by store and category.

## Objective
Build a dashboard to monitor:
- Sales
- Expenses
- Profit
- Trends

## Analysis
Data was cleaned and analyzed using Excel, SQL and Power BI.

## Key Findings
- Baku store generates highest revenue
- Food category drives most sales
- Expenses increase in March reducing margin

## Recommendations
- Control operational expenses
- Increase drinks marketing
- Focus on high-performing stores
